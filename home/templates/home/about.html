About us

Furnitart is a small furniture company based in Dublin, Ireland.

All our products are designed and produced by us. Our main goal is to create high quality products with a very essential and functional design.
Our furniture has minimalist style and is extremely easy to asseble. We only work with first quality materials.
If you want good quality but you are not ready to spend big money, Furnitart is the shop for you!
